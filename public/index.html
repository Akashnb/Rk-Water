What's Inside

R.K. Alkaline Water is enriched with essential minerals that contribute to your overall health and well-being. Here’s a detailed look at the mineral composition of our water:

Calcium

5-12 mg/dm³

Calcium is vital for maintaining strong bones and teeth. It also plays a crucial role in muscle function and nerve signaling.

Magnesium

2-5 mg/dm³

Magnesium helps regulate muscle and nerve function, blood sugar levels, and blood pressure. It also supports the production of protein, bone, and DNA.

Sodium

20-25 mg/dm³

Sodium is essential for maintaining fluid balance, muscle contraction, and nerve transmission.

Chlorine

~46 mg/dm³

Chlorine in controlled amounts helps maintain water purity by eliminating harmful bacteria and pathogens.

Sourness (pH Level)

6.8-7.3

The slightly alkaline pH level of our water helps neutralize acidity in the body, promoting overall health and wellness.

Mineralization

90-140 mg/dm³
